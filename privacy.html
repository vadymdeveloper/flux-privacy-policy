Flux

Privacy Policy

Effective July 20, 2026 · Flux: Finance Tracker (com.vadymdev.flux)

Overview

Flux ("we", "our", "the app") helps you track spending, budgets, debts, crypto holdings, and financial goals. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. The short version: your financial data is entered by you, stored under your own account, never sold, and never used for advertising.

Information We Collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo via Firebase Authentication. We never see your Google password and cannot access your other Google services.
  • Financial data you enter. Transactions, budgets, debts, accounts, crypto holdings, recurring payments, and goals. All of it is entered manually by you — Flux does not connect to your bank and cannot read balances from anywhere else.
  • Usage and diagnostics. Limited app usage events, device and app diagnostics, and crash reports via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics. Analytics events never include financial amounts, notes, names, email addresses, or transaction and debt identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your account information to create and secure your Flux profile, sync your data across devices, and personalize the app. Usage and diagnostic information helps us understand feature adoption, investigate errors, and improve reliability. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your data for advertising.

Third-Party Services

Flux fetches live crypto prices from CoinGecko and currency exchange rates from ExchangeRate-API. These requests contain no personal or financial data, but like any web request they expose your IP address to those services. Authentication, data storage, analytics, and crash reporting are provided by Google Firebase. None of these services are used to serve advertising.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), scoped to your account and protected by security rules so only you can read it. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest and may be processed on Google servers outside your country under industry-standard safeguards. App preferences are included in encrypted Android device backups tied to your own Google account.

Analytics & Diagnostics

Firebase Analytics records privacy-safe product events such as screen views, selected currency, and transaction or debt action types. Firebase Crashlytics records crashes, non-fatal errors, stack traces, app version, device model, operating system, and recent analytics breadcrumbs.

Data Retention

We keep your data only while your account exists. When you delete your account, your financial data is permanently removed from our systems. Crash and analytics records are retained by Firebase for a limited period defined by Google, then deleted automatically.

Delete Your Account & Data

In the app: Settings → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and all financial data stored in Flux.

Without the app: email dev.vadymhrynyk@gmail.com from the Google account address you signed in with, and we will delete your account and data.

Your Rights

You can review, update, export (CSV), or delete everything from within the app. You may also request access, correction, or deletion by email. If you are in the EU or UK, you additionally have the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

Children's Privacy

Flux is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Flux evolves. Material changes will be communicated in-app before they take effect.
